About

I’m Dorothy Addie, but I go by my last name Addie. I’m a programmer and fiber artist based in Brooklyn, New York. I currently work at The New York Stem Cell Foundation, where I use machine learning and statistical methods to drive disease research.

Aside from working in tech, I love designing knitwear, teaching others various forms of fiber crafts, and building community spaces in Brooklyn.
